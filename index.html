What is Niyasha?

Niyasha is an organization started way back in 2023. We organized workshops to connect children who are in restrained circumstances to the world of art. Now we are back again and I need your help to find children for the next workshop!

Where? Bhilai | भिलाई

How Long? 2 years

"Through our platform, children can express their artistic view of the world, which is not taught through normal mediums of our society. Our initiative has connected 15+ students and we hope to acheive this every year."
